How your price is calculated

American West uses one rating pipeline for every service tier. Your quote breakdown shows each step in plain language:

  1. Cube from dimensions — each item: (length × width × height in inches) ÷ 1,728 = cubic feet; shipment total = sum of all lines × quantity.
  2. Rate group — total cube is placed in a bracket (<251 · 251–500 · 501–1,000 · 1,001–2,000 · 2,001–5,000 cf). The largest bracket requires a custom quote.
  3. Linehaul charge — per-pound rate from your lane/tier and rate group × shipment weight (lbs). If the lane’s minimum charge is higher, the minimum applies instead.
  4. Fuel surcharge — linehaul charge × current fuel percentage (updated weekly from diesel indices).
  5. Insurance — 1% of declared value with a $25 minimum; $0 if you enter no declared value.
  6. Accessorials — optional services such as residential delivery or lift gate, added to the total.

Each service tier (Threshold, White Glove No Inspection, White Glove Inspection) runs the same steps with a different rate table — that is why tier prices differ for the same shipment.

Common terms

Declared value insurance
1% of your declared shipment value, with a $25 minimum charge. Enter $0 declared value to omit this line.
CWT
A pricing method based on shipment weight. "CWT" means "per hundredweight" (100 lbs).
Cube
A pricing method based on shipment volume (cubic feet), often used for bulky but light freight.
Fuel Surcharge
A variable charge tied to current diesel fuel prices, updated weekly from industry indices.
Accessorial
An additional service charge — for example residential delivery, lift gate service, or extra-person assistance.
Call for Quote
A destination that requires a custom quote rather than an instant online price. Our team will contact you with a tailored rate.

Declared value & insurance

When you enter a declared value, your quote includes an insurance charge of 1% of declared value, with a $25 minimum. If declared value is $0, no insurance charge is added.

Example: $18,000 declared value → 1% = $180.00 insurance (above the $25 floor).

Standard coverage terms still apply up to the lower of $500 per article or $1 per pound unless additional coverage is arranged.

Excluded or specialty items — statuary, fine art, rare antiques, and one-of-a-kind items may not be fully covered under standard terms. Please confirm with our team before shipping high-value or specialty goods.
