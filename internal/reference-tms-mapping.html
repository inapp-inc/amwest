AlphaTruck Rate-Key Mapping

Maps platform service types to AlphaTruck tariff codes (demo XX1/XX2 B2B, YY1/YY2 HD), MCTC level codes, and BW/BX/BY service keys. CSV exports upload via FileZilla to AlphaTruck.

AlphaTruck codes (demo): B2B uses XX1 (rate) + XX2 (minimum). Home delivery uses YY1 + YY2. Service types: BW (B2B), BX (Threshold), BY (White Glove). MCTC level (e.g. DEMO-9001) is read-only from tariff.
