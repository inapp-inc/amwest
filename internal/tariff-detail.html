TAR-B2B-BASE

National B2B — CWT v35 · Active

National business-to-business rate program (per-pound v35 breaks). Separate from White Glove Inspection — see TAR-WGI-BASE for WGI.

Effective Jan 1, 2026 — Dec 31, 2026

Clone & edit in configurator Delete
How a quote uses this tariff:
  1. Rep selects customer and lane — system matches this tariff automatically.
  2. Base rate comes from the origin × service table (or rate tables by origin).
  3. Customer discount, lane override, and commodity rules adjust the linehaul.
  4. Fuel and accessorials are added from Pricing Setup — not stored on this page.
